Introduction to SSBC

SSBC is a booming construction supply firm based in Eastern Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to helping you take significant steps toward building your dream place. We provide an extensive range of high-quality construction supplies, ensuring that you have everything you need to bring your vision to life. Partnering with renowned brands like ITPL , JK CEMENT , JK BLOCKS , CLAVECONE BLOCKS ,INFRA BLOCKS ,ORGANO and KAMDHENU , we deliver products that stand the test of time, reflecting our commitment to excellence and reliability.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, SSBC has quickly established itself as a prominent organization in the wholesale trade of construction materials. Our journey began with a simple yet profound mission: to bridge the gap between manufacturers and consumers by providing top-notch construction supplies and unparalleled customer service. Over the years, we have expanded our horizons, continuously striving to meet the evolving needs of our clients and the construction industry.

What we offer

At SSBC, we offer a diverse range of products designed to cater to various construction needs. Our product lineup includes:

  • AAC Blocks: Lightweight, durable, and energy-efficient blocks for modern construction.
  • Block Adhesive: High-quality adhesive for strong and reliable bonding of blocks.
  • Tiles and Tile Adhesive: A variety of tiles and adhesive solutions for perfect finishes.
  • Ready-Mix Plaster with Waterproofing Solution: Easy-to-use plaster that ensures durability and protection.
  • Trade and Non-Trade Cement: Versatile cement options for different construction requirements.
  • Emulsions SS1 and RS1: High-performance emulsions for various applications.
  • Road Marking Paints: Durable and visible paints for road safety and marking.
